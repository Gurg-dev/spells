Sutekh City

City established in the Valley of Sand next to the inner sea of Mordia. Sutekh was captured by Bata's forces some years ago. Since then, Bata has lead the city with his evil minions - ruling with an iron fist. First adventurers that arrived there were hauling a mysterious artifact for Bata. City council has been plotting the demice of Bata. The party of people with help of Mantis Guild in the city is planning to overthrow the unrightful tyrant. Their arrival started a series of events leading to Bata using Artifacts only known to him to free his mother from her higher domain. Now Bata's mother is free in Mordia, and the story continues...

Ealdthryn

Alignment: Chaotic Good
Domains: Earth, Protection, Knowledge

Symbol: Book Garb: Staff, Long robes Favored Weapons: Staff Typical Worshipers: Good humanoids, good monks,philosophers, planar creatures In the pantheon of the gods. Ealdthryn is known as the deity of wisdom and knowledge. It is said that Ealdthryn's vast intellect is matched only by their insatiable hunger for learning and understanding. Ealdthryn is often depicted as a wise old figure with a long white beard, deep-set eyes, and a serene expression. They are typically depicted holding a staff or book, symbolizing their mastery over knowledge. According to legend, Ealdthryn was one of the first beings to emerge from the void at the beginning of creation. As the universe took shape around them, Ealdthryn set about cataloging and studying every aspect of it, from the movement of the stars to the behavior of the smallest subatomic particles. Ealdthryn's quest for knowledge was unquenchable, and they spent countless eons traveling the cosmos, exploring every corner of it. Over time, Ealdthryn became revered by mortal beings for their vast knowledge and wisdom. Temples were built in their honor, and they were worshipped by scholars, sages, and philosophers alike. Ealdthryn was seen as a source of guidance and inspiration, and their followers sought their counsel on matters of science, philosophy, and magic. Ealdthryn's teachings are focused on the importance of learning and self-improvement.They believe that the pursuit of knowledge is a sacred duty, and that every being has the potential to grow and expand their understanding of the universe. Ealdthryn's followers are encouraged to seek out new experiences, learn from their mistakes, and constantly strive to better themselves. Despite their immense wisdom, Ealdthryn is not infallible. There are many stories of Ealdthryn making mistakes or underestimating the power of their own creations. However, these missteps are seen as learning opportunities, and Ealdthryn is always quick to rectify their errors and use them to expand their understanding of the universe. In combat, Ealdthryn is rarely directly involved, preferring instead to use their vast intellect to outmaneuver and outwit their opponents. Ealdthryn's followers are often powerful mages, using their knowledge of the arcane to manipulate reality itself and gain an edge in battle. Ealdthryn's enemies are those who seek to suppress knowledge and keep others in ignorance. They view such beings as threats to the natural order of the universe, and will go to great lengths to thwart their plans. Ealdthryn's followers are also sworn enemies of any who seek to use knowledge for personal gain or to subjugate others.
